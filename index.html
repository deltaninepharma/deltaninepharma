About Us

Delta-Nine Pharmaceuticals is a South African company based in the Western Cape. We specialize in EU-GMP compliant post-harvest processing of medical cannabis. Our focus is on quality, compliance, and reliability in delivering cannabis flower and extracts for the global medical market.

Our Services

  • Trimming and sorting of cannabis flower
  • Drying and curing to pharmaceutical standards
  • Gamma irradiation for microbial decontamination
  • Packaging and labeling under EU-GMP protocols
  • Extraction from cannabis trim and waste (coming soon)

Compliance

Our operations are aligned with SAHPRA and EU-GMP guidelines. We work with certified local and European compliance consultants to ensure every step of our process meets international standards. This includes documentation, validation, facility design, and quality control protocols.
